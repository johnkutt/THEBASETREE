# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is TheBaseTree?

TheBaseTree is a green credit marketplace on Base Chain enabling trading and retirement of carbon credits.

## How do I buy credits?

Connect your wallet, browse listings, and purchase with ETH or USDC.

## How do I retire credits?

Go to the Retirement page, select credits, and confirm retirement. You'll receive an NFT proof.

## What blockchain is used?

Base Chain (Ethereum L2) for low fees and fast settlement.

## Are the credits verified?

Yes, all credits are verified against major standards like VCS and Gold Standard.
